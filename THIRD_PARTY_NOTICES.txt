libplacebo, and the other runtime dependencies are statically linked into that
replaceable libmpv library by the reproducible source build script. Copyright
notices and complete license texts are included in the application's Resources
directory; the pinned upstream source archives are identified by the bundled
runtime manifest. The application does not use user-installed mpv, FFmpeg, or
Homebrew libraries.
